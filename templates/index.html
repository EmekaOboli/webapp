ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE / AUTOMATION

In the past 2 years, the need for A I and Automation in Legal Practise has been a topic for symposium conversions around the world. In Africa, the Nigerian Bar Association at the 2019 NBA Annual General Conference held in Lagos, Nigeria had a special break out session focused on A I and Tech. our Firm took particular interest in this session. we intend to build and programme software codes using PYTHON PROGRAMING LANGUAGE for automation of multiple petitions and cases which ordinarily would require staffers, time and energy to complete.

The Nigerian Judiciary (with particular regards to the Abuja, F.C.T Judicial Division) has a big problem with case assignment, re-assignment and management generally; we will solve this problem with A I and Automation. our solution will be replicated through out state judiciaries, providing quality and seamless access to justice for all Nigerians. Lawyers will find A I useful.
